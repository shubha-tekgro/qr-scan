Welcome to Your Guided Tour

Patan Durbar Square is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Lalitpur, Nepal. Built during the Malla period, it represents centuries of cultural, artistic, and religious traditions.

The square contains palaces, temples, statues, and courtyards, showcasing exquisite Newar craftsmanship. Visitors scanning this QR code can explore the rich history and architecture in detail.

Quick Timeline
